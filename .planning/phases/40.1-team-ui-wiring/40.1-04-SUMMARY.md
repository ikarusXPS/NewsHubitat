# Phase 40.1 Plan 04: E2E Tests for Team Wiring Flows — Summary

**One-liner:** 4 serial Playwright E2E tests appended to `teams.spec.ts` proving the 4 Phase 28 debt-payback flows work end-to-end through real browser + real backend.

## Tests Added

| # | Test name | What it proves | Skip condition |
|---|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| 1 | `saves a bookmark to a team via dropdown` | BookmarkButton ChevronDown trigger + portal dropdown team option + team dashboard shows the team name | No article cards with team-bookmark dropdown visible |
| 2 | `team bookmark anchor has target=_blank and non-fallback href` | TeamBookmarkCard anchor has `target="_blank"` and `href` does NOT match `/article/` fallback | No `.glass-panel a[target="_blank"]` anchors present (no team bookmarks) |
